Meet Krishna

I built this practice on one idea — real estate should feel personal.

For years I've worked across both sides of Saskatoon real estate — helping families find homes they love, and helping investors look after the assets they've worked hard to build. The two sides of my work feed each other. I see this market from every angle.

My approach is simple. Be reachable. Be honest about what I see. Move fast when timing matters and slow when it shouldn't be rushed. Treat every property — whether you're buying it, selling it, or trusting me to manage it — like it's my own.

Saskatoon is home. I know the neighbourhoods, the builders, the lenders, the rental market, and the quirks. If you're buying, selling, renting or owning here, I'd like to be the person you call.
